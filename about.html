About Us

Built around dependable quality, clear communication, and school-first production.

B&T Apparel is a South African school uniform manufacturer established in 2021, focused on durable, affordable uniform supply for primary and secondary schools.

Background

Why we started

B&T Apparel was established to provide reliable access to high-quality school uniforms that align with each school’s colours, branding requirements, and practical everyday wear standards.

Quality

Manufacturing with control

Strict quality control measures are applied throughout the manufacturing process to ensure consistency, durability, and a professional finish.

Vision

Leading and trusted

To become a leading and trusted school uniform manufacturer in South Africa, known for quality, reliability, and excellent customer service.

Mission

School-standard production

To manufacture high-quality school uniforms that meet school standards and customer expectations, while ensuring timely delivery and fair pricing.

Growth

Capacity and reach

We aim to grow through stronger partnerships, increased production capacity, and steady regional and national expansion.

Service

Made-to-order and bulk

Our operating model supports both custom production and bulk manufacturing for schools, SGBs, parents, and learners.

Values

The standards that guide every order

  • Quality and attention to detail
  • Integrity and transparency
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Reliability and accountability
  • Continuous improvement
